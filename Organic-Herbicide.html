Aagya - ORGANIC HERBICIDES
AAGYA (Orobanche weed control)

Content:

Linoleic acid 0.5 %, diterpenoids 0.3%, glycosides 0.3%, Phenolic compounds 1%.

Mode of Action:

Natural constituents in Aagya cause immediate death of Orobanche weed roots. It is also beneficial for host plant root growth. Aagya does not cause any harm effect to host plant.

Application and Dosage: When Orobanche weed starts to grow, Spray Over the plant or drenching in plant roots.

Crops: Rapeseed (Mustard), Tomatoes, Tobacco, and other orobanche infested crops.

Dosage: 500 ML per acre by spray or drenching.

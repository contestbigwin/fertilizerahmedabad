Nutro-N (Organic Nitrogen)

Content:

Nitrogen 60000 ppm

Potassium 20000 ppm

Organic Carbon 14%

Valid as per Fertilizer Control Order, India's grade of Liquid Fermented Organic Manure

For vegetative growth and long lasting greenery of plants, we have a better option compared to urea or any other chemical nitrogen fertilizer. This fertilizer is cheaper in cost and has almost double efficiency. Organic Nitrogen fertilizer gives protection against fungal diseases, yellowing of leaves and plants.

Mode of Action:

Nutro-N is useful for stimulation of plant microbiomes which perform increased nutrient uptake. Sarvo-N play key role as biostimulant through the modulation of plant molecular and physiological processes that trigger growth and increase yield. Also acts for stimulation of carbon and nitrogen metabolism.

Application: Can be sprayed over plant or applied with irrigation on growth stage of any crop.

Dosage: 500 ML per acre by foliar spray or 1 Litre per acre through irrigation.

Nutro-P (Organic Phosphate)

Content:

P2O5 12%

Calcium 40000 ppm

Organic Carbon 14%

Valid as per Fertilizer Control Order, India's grade of Liquid Fermented Organic Manure

For better seed germination, root development, more number of shoots and flowers, organic phosphate is highly efficient because it gets 100% available to plants. This fertilizer do not get stable in soil like chemical phosphates.

Mode of Action:

Organic Phosphate stimulates root development which helps plant to uptake nutrients from soil. Organic phosphate is useful for fruiting and maturity at right time. Organic Phosphate supports in genes formation and development, and transfer of genes from one generation to next generation.

Application: Can be applied through irrigation at plantation time, and sprayed at flowering stage in all crops. Also can be used for seed treatment for high germination rate.

Dosage: : 1 Litre per acre by spray or irrigation.

Nutro-K (Organic Potassium)

Content:

K2O 12%

Organic Carbon 14%

Valid as per Fertilizer Control Order, India's grade of Liquid Fermented Organic Manure

For water retention in plants, disease resistance, fruit setting, size and shining; Organic Potassium is useful as best yield enhancer.

Mode of Action:

Organic Potassium is helpful in movement of water, nutrients and carbohydrates in plant tissues. Potassium regulates opening and closing of stomata. It is useful for water retention, reducing respiration, and enhances translocation of sugar and starch. Potassium increases plants' protein content and helps disease resistance.

Application:Spray or soil application with irrigation at fruit setting and maturity stage.

Dosage: :1 Litre per acre by spray or irrigation.

ABOUT US

"It is said that-You become what you eat or drink!"

Standards of soil and plant health mainly depends on fertigation. That’s the reason Fertilizer Ahmedabad focuses on natural organic fertilizers in different forms.

Having 27 years of experience in fertilizer industry, we have essence of fertilizer in different soil type, environment, and crop pattern.

The best part of our product development is liquid and granulated form of fertilizer with nutrition, disease protectant and pest-insect protectant properties, which are never before formulations.

We produce Nitrogen, Phosphate, Potassium, Secondary nutrients, anti-fungal, anti-virus, anti-nematode, pest and insect repellent fertilizers in purely organic form with Organic Carbon and no heavy metals or chemicals in it.

We produce fertilizer products for different companies as per their requirement to be marketed in their own brands.

Our Vision

we aim to preserve the environment and promote long-term agricultural sustainability.
To revolutionize the fertilizer industry by providing natural organic fertilizers in various forms that promote soil and plant health, while ensuring environmental sustainability and the well-being of farmers and consumer

Our Mission

Our core mission is to deliver state-of-the-art, research and development-backed solutions to farmers, empowering them with the latest advancements in agriculture. We are dedicated to providing quick and efficient solutions through the utilization of cutting-edge technology, extensive knowledge, and a range of high-quality, reasonably priced products. Furthermore, we are committed to conducting our operations in an environmentally and socially responsible manner, aiming to contribute to a better quality of life for society as a whole.
